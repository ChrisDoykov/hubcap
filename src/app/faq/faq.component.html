Q: What are CPS?

A: Cyber-Physical Systems are systems that link the physical world (e.g., through sensors or actuators) with the digital world of software systems and communications technology. A CPS will often include components from many different providers, so your business may already be contributing towards CPSs.

There is growing demand for autonomous, secure CPSs that can interact with humans while respecting privacy. This creates opportunities for SMEs in an ever-wider range of domains, but in order to take them, businesses must leverage advances in engineering technology across sectors and regions. Model-Based Design (MBD) tools help you represent and analyse products digitally before realising them in hardware and software, by building digital twins and using simulation for collaborative diagnostics and decision making.

Q: What is Model-Based Design (MBD) ?

A: HUBCAP helps you to adopt digital tools that allow you to model, simulate, analyse and visualise products before you build them.

These tools help you to anticipate solutions, sidestep unwanted emergent properties, and analyse costs in a more efficient way than pencil-and-paper methods, or “build it and see”. Using modern, comprehensive model-based engineering tools can speed up and de-risk the transformation from design to product. Through HUBCAP’s services and platform, we will help your business find the right model-based engineering tools for the job, support your up-skilling, and provide a chance to experiment with the latest tools in a “sand-pit” environment.

HUBCAP is a one-stop-shop to help your European SME – whatever your sector and wherever you are situated – to undertake digital innovation using new model-based design technology for Cyber- Physical Systems.

To learn more about what we offer see our Services Services page.

What Are CPS

Cyber-Physical Systems are systems that link the physical world (e.g., through sensors or actuators) with the digital world of software systems and communications technology. A CPS will often include components from many different providers, so your business may already be contributing towards CPSs.
Learn More Learn More

Model-Based Design

Model-Based Design (MBD) is a method to address the design of complex systems with models. It is quite standard in software engineering and is becoming more and more relevant in system engineering as well.
